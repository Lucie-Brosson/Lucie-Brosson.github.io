Last Century Chronicles

Date : January 2023

Medium : Drawing, Photoshop, Indesign, Javascript, JSON

Last century Chronicles is a role play game inspired by the mythology of Lovecraft. The tales appens in Paris in the catacombs and the 18th borough. To better the participation of the players, I created a discord bot. The bot can throw dice and keep in memory the levels of the players. I also did the graphics and illustration of the project.
